Frequently asked questions

What is the format and length of each class?

Your child's live, online, 1-hour class is taught by their own personal teacher who encourages and guides them through instruction, demonstration, and games.

What class schedules are available?

The beauty of BIDDABD FutureSchool is the incredible flexibility. Easily create a class schedule that best suits you and your child. If a conflict comes up, not to worry! We understand that life happens. We require students to attend at least 4 classes per month, but beyond that, the frequency of classes is up to you. Cancel and reschedule any class within 24 hours of your class start time without penalty.

Can I rebook the trial class if I missed it?

Absolutely!

Can children learn a musical instrument online?

Yes! We do three things to make that happen:
(1) Use a custom-built curriculum created specifically for online classes
(2) Provide trained instructors to deliver engaging, impactful, fun online classes
(3) Use a custom platform for music that supports resources like backing tracks and sheet music

Does my child need an instrument to enroll in the course?

The great news is, no instrument is needed for the trial class or for the first 4 classes of the "Discovery" level. However, your child will need an instrument going forward. An acoustic guitar is required for the guitar program. For piano, at least a 61-key touch-sensitive keyboard is required. BYJU’S FutureSchool can help you source an instrument if needed.

How does BIDDABD FutureSchool handle refunds?

We have an anytime refund policy.

At what times are classes available?

Classes are available at regular times in your time zone.
