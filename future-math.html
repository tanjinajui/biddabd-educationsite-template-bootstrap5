Frequently asked questions

What are the courses like?

BIDDABD FutureSchool teaches math concepts through real-life applications that are highly visual and interactive. Learning is aided by post-class quizzes and practice worksheets. Our students also get to experience our unique Capstone Projects designed to solve real-world problems through math, and our exclusive math championships.

What curriculum does BIDDABD FutureSchool teach?

BIDDABD FutureSchool's curriculum is based on the US Common Core Standards for math.

Will these math courses help improve my child's overall math abilities?

Our courses are designed to help improve overall math abilities and build math confidence in the classroom and in life. Our online math curriculum is designed around research that recommends encouraging a math mindset in children through projects and play, to foster a love of math.

What class schedules are available?

The beauty of BIDDABD FutureSchool is the incredible flexibility. Easily create a class schedule that best suits you. If a conflict comes up, not to worry! We understand that life happens. We require students to attend at least 4 classes per month, but beyond that, the frequency of classes is up to you. Cancel and reschedule any class within 24 hours of your class start time without penalty.

Can a student enroll in other courses in addition to math?

Yes, a student can enroll in any or all of the courses we offer.

What happens in a math trial class?

In the trial class, your child gets a chance to explore the amazing world of math through exciting activities with an engaging teacher. The class is a brief introduction to grade-specific math concepts. The teacher will also assess your child’s understanding of math.

Can I rebook the trial class if I missed it?

Absolutely!
