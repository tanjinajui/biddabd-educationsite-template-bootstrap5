Frequently asked questions

Is the coding curriculum similar to what is taught in schools?

Yes, the coding curriculum is similar to what is taught in schools.

What’s the ideal age to start coding?

Kids in grade 1 can start learning coding with us. Learning to code at a young age is a great opportunity for kids and teens to develop problem-solving and critical-thinking skills while building the necessary focus and organization to see projects through to completion.

Which coding languages are covered in coding courses?

Coding usually begins with drag-and-drop visual programming, where kids connect blocks to make programs. Visual programming teaches the fundamental concepts without typing or syntax and focuses on the logic behind coding.
Once they've learned the basics, kids can transition to real-world programming languages like Python and C.

What is the format and length of each class?

Each class is live and online, with an instructor to teach and guide the student. The duration of each class is 1 hour.

Can courses be taken on a phone or tablet?

No, at this time, courses cannot be taken on a phone or tablet. All classes must be taken on a laptop or desktop computer.

How does BIDDABD FutureSchool handle refunds?

We have an anytime refund policy.

At what times are classes available?

Classes are available at regular times in your time zone.
